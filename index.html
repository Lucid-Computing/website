SUPER CONFIDENTIAL

The Architecture of Trust

01

Verifiable Agent Pods

Agents as Colleagues, Governed as Machines

Your AI agent gets a full identity — email, workspace presence, shared docs — just like onboarding a new teammate. Underneath, the Operator injects governance sidecars into a single hardware-encrypted pod where the CPU itself prevents anyone — even Lucid — from accessing its memory, credentials, or data.

02

Auditor Chain & Gateway

Sidecar Guardrails, One Cedar Decision

Every request flows from the model through a chain of auditor sidecars — Guardrails, PII, Sovereignty, Eval — each producing typed claims about the traffic. The Gateway collects all claims and evaluates a single Cedar policy to allow, deny, or warn. One policy, one decision per request — no scattered logic.

03

Verifier & AI Passport

Cryptographic Proof, Outside the Pod

The Gateway bundles all claims, the Cedar decision, and a TEE signature into signed evidence — then sends it to the Lucid Verifier service outside the pod. The Verifier appraises the evidence against hardware quotes and issues an AI Passport: tamper-proof, independently verifiable cryptographic proof of everything the agent did.
